About Me

I am a PhD Candidate in Computer Science at University Jean Monnet in Saint-Etienne (France) since 2015. I am working on the topic of "Fraud Anomaly Detection for financial application" under the supervision of Pr. Marc Sebban, Pr. Amaury Habrard and Pr. Elisa Fromont.

I am doing this PhD under a CIFRE contract with the company Blitz Business Service, in which I am working as Data Scientist. I really want to thank them for giving me the opportunity to this thesis !

Research Interests

Supervised Learning, Imbalanced Learning, Cost-Sensitive Learning, Optimization and Statistical Learning Theory.

Previously

I am graduated from the University of Lyon in Applied Mathematics (Master Degree) in Biology and Medicine and from the University of Strasbourg In Fundamental Mathematics (Magistère), both in 2014.

During my Master Degree, I did a research internship at INRIA Lyon under the supervision of Pr. Fabien Crauste and Pr. Olivier Gandrillon. The topic of my internship was: Contribution to the study of the variability of CD8 T Immune Response.
